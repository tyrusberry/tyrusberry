Sawtooth Region

The sawtooth region of level m is 2^m triangles arranged with their bases along the unit interval (analagous the base of the mth level Sierpinski gasket) with an overlap of (2^(-m))/10 introduced and a specified height.

[p,t,v,l]=sawtooth(Level,height,# of Refinements);
